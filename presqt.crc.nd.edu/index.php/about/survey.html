Needs Assessment

Participants were invited to contribute answers for the PresQT research study, entitled “Data and Software Preservation Quality Tool Needs Assessment”. In the Summer of 2017 PresQT conducted a needs analysis related to data and software preservation. Participant's answers contribute to a better understanding of what tools and/or tool suites would be of benefit those who aim to better preserve data and software.

Data Collection closed Sept 1, 2017 at 5 PM EDT.

The PresQT Questionnaire and de-identified data are accessible, along with the other PresQT resources shared on OSF.

PresQT Needs Assessment results' Data Visualizations are accessible online.
